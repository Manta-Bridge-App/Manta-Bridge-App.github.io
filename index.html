Runbook: Consistent Results with Manta Bridge

Before You Start (Manta Network)

  • Hold native gas on both ends for approvals and finalize steps.
  • Verify token addresses; fee-on-transfer tokens can affect minReceived.
  • Test with a micro-bridge; confirm destination balance, then scale in tranches.

Slippage & Gas for Manta Bridge

  • Deep stables/majors: 0.10–0.50% slippage.
  • Mid-caps/average depth: 0.50–1.50% depending on venue/liquidity.
  • Illiquid: 1.50%+; split size or pivot via deeper pairs post-finality.

Troubleshooting — Manta Bridge

  • Pending too long: bump priority fee or switch RPC; avoid peak volatility.
  • Reverted tx / insufficient output: refresh quotes, modestly widen slippage, or split order.
  • Exit delay: track progress via the chosen route’s finality instead of resubmitting duplicates.
